Plain Language

Know Your Local Laws

Understand the ordinances that affect your daily life, explained in simple and accessible language.

🌿 Environment
Claveria Solid Waste Management Ordinance (Ord. No. 2025-07)
Every household must separate garbage into biodegradable (food scraps, leaves) and non-biodegradable (plastics, metals) before collection day. Failure to segregate can result in a ₱500–₱2,000 fine.
In Effect
🏍️ Transport
Tricycle Franchise & Operations Ordinance (Ord. No. 2025-06)
If you operate a tricycle for hire, you need a franchise from the SB, renewed every year. Your tricycle must display your franchise number and follow assigned routes. Unauthorized routes can result in confiscation.
In Effect
🎣 Fisheries
Coastal & Marine Resources Protection (Ord. No. 2025-04)
Commercial fishing boats are prohibited within 15 kilometers of the Claveria coastline. Only municipal fishermen with valid licenses may fish in these protected zones. Illegal fishing using dynamite or cyanide carries criminal penalties.
In Effect
👥 Social
Women & Children Protection Ordinance (Ord. No. 2025-05)
Domestic violence is illegal and punishable under local law. Victims may seek help from the Barangay VAW Desk, the MSWD, or the Municipal Police Station. All reports are handled confidentially.
In Effect
🎓 Youth & Education
Youth Scholarship Program (Resolution No. 2025-18)
Qualified Claveria residents aged 18–24 enrolled in college may apply for a scholarship grant covering tuition and a monthly ₱1,500 stipend. Priority given to honor students and those from low-income families.
Active
🏥 Health
Anti-Smoking Ordinance in Public Places (Ord. No. 2024-18)
Smoking is strictly prohibited in all government offices, schools, health centers, markets, and public transport terminals. Designated smoking areas are provided in specified locations. Violators face a fine of ₱200 for the first offense.
In Effect

Ordinance No. 2025-07

An Ordinance Establishing the Claveria Solid Waste Management Program

EnvironmentApproved: Feb 26, 2025In Effect

Plain Language Summary

This ordinance requires every household, business, and institution in Claveria to separate garbage into three categories: (1) biodegradable waste, (2) non-biodegradable waste, and (3) special or hazardous waste.

Non-compliance is subject to fines of ₱500 for the first offense, ₱1,000 for the second, and ₱2,000 for subsequent violations.

What this means for you:

Prepare three separate bins at home. Your barangay will post collection schedules.
